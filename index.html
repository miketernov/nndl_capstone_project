    About the project

    NutritionAI uses a lightweight CNN trained on Nutrition5k and runs entirely in the browser with TensorFlow.js. It combines personalized calorie planning with AI-based meal analysis.

    Features

    • Personalized calorie & macro targets
    • AI-based nutrient estimation from food photos
    • Daily dashboard & analytics
    • Meal type tracking (breakfast/lunch/dinner/snack)
    • Browser push notifications
    • Persistent profile & history (localStorage)
    • Light/dark theme
    • CSV/JSON export
